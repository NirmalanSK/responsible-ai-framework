human_decision_verifier.py · ethics_code.py · S09b ✨
Causal Human Oversight Verifier — Step 09b
"Who watches the watchman?" · Pearl L3 · post EXPERT_REVIEW only · ACCEPT / RE_ESCALATE
▶
Problem it solves: After EXPERT_REVIEW escalation, human reviewer decisions were accepted unconditionally — single point of accountability failure. A fatigued or biased reviewer could ALLOW high-risk content with no check.

3-component verifier (verify_human_decision()):

① PearlCounterfactualVerifier (L3 do-calculus):
counterfactual_delta = P(harm|do(ALLOW)) − P(harm|do(BLOCK))
P(harm|do(ALLOW)) ≈ scm_risk/100 · P(harm|do(BLOCK)) ≈ scm_risk × 0.05
delta > 0.55 → RE_ESCALATE (human decision causally critical)

② RiskGapAnalyzer:
gap = |AI risk score − human implied risk|
ALLOW → implied risk ≈ 0.10 · BLOCK → implied risk ≈ 0.90
gap > 0.60 → RE_ESCALATE (AI and human strongly disagree)

③ ConstitutionalEthicsChecker (5 principles):
HARM_PREVENTION ALLOW + risk ≥ 80% → CRITICAL violation
FAIRNESS Protected-attribute query + no documented reason
AUTONOMY BLOCK + risk < 20% → unjustified suppression
TRANSPARENCY Large gap + no reason → EU AI Act Art.14 violation
ACCOUNTABILITY Single reviewer ALLOW on risk ≥ 70% → senior review required

Outcome logic: RE_ESCALATE if cf_delta > 0.55 OR gap > 0.60 OR CRITICAL violation OR HIGH violation + no reason. ACCEPT otherwise (violations logged in audit trail).

Files: ethics_code.py (EthicsCode, HumanReviewInput, EthicsViolation) + human_decision_verifier.py (all 3 components + orchestrator) + pipeline_v15.py (verify_human_decision() method, self.s09b, print_report() extended)

Tests: TestHumanDecisionVerifier — 17 new tests · 195 → 212/212 ✅

Extends Stoyanovich et al. accountability + transparency to the human oversight layer. Year 2: DoWhy replaces structural approximation — same interface, zero pipeline changes."
